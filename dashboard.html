Edge = mean(outcome − price) per trade; positive = systematically bought the winning side below fair value (alpha). t‑stat ranks by how reliable that edge is given sample size — it replaces eyeballing the trade count. Only fills in fully-resolved markets are scored (edge needs a settled outcome). The window selects bets whose market resolved within that lookback — so 1h/24h show who's hot on recently-settled markets, while 1y/all show long-run skill. Wallets are not deduplicated and resolved markets are backward-looking.
